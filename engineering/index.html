01

Thermacell Repellents: Impact Tester

Thermacell Repellents designs spatial mosquito repellers for use in various outdoor environments. The repellers heat a chemical repellent causing it to disperse, resulting in a mosquito-free zone. Over time, these repellers are exposed to wear and tear including being dropped, knocked over, and hit accidentally. During my co-op at this company, I was tasked with designing a custom fixture to recreate common impact forces that a repeller would be exposed to. The reference force I used was a device being struck by a soccer ball.

Insulet Corporation: GEE Mechanical Design Co-op

Insulet Corporation manufactures the Omnipod, a tubeless insulin delivery system for people with diabetes.
