Photography

Travel has been one of the most formative parts of my life outside of engineering. Experiencing different cultures, landscapes, and ways of living has given me a perspective I couldn't get from a classroom. Photography is how I hold onto those experiences. It forces me to slow down, pay attention to what's around me, and notice details I'd otherwise walk right past. What started as a way to document trips has turned into something I genuinely look forward to every time I'm somewhere new.
